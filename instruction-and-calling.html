Classes and Workshops

I offer classes and workshops lasting from 30 minutes to several days, covering a variety of subjects related to dance. Specializing in historic, folk dance and social dance from many different periods and cultures, I work with everyone from pre-schoolers to seniors offering private, couple and group instruction. Some of my classes and workshops include Wedding Dance Preparation, Family/Intergenerational Dance, Team Building, Play-Party Games, Irish Set Dancing, dancing with Special Populations and dancing for Healing. Workshops are popular with camps, scouts, spiritual retreats, library programs, senior citizen groups, parties, youth and homeschool groups. Read more

Dance Calling

Contra  ~  Family/Intergenerational  ~  English Country  ~  Appalachian  ~   Irish Sets  ~ Barn Dancing

“I love your calling! You’re always so clear and choose dances with a difference. I wish you called here every week.”

The traditional dances I call use movements that have been passed down through generations. Most are based on walking and are easy enough for a person of any age to learn. For more experienced dancers, I select dances with challenge to keep the mind as active as the body.

My calling is perfect for a variety of dances, dance weekends, dance camps, conferences, retreats, weddings, celebrations and youth groups. Read more
