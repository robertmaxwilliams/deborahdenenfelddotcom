Grant Writing

For the last twenty years much of my income as a dancer and dance educator has been funded through the support of grants. These grants come from both public and private sources and are obtained through my efforts as a grant writer.

My analytical mind and curiosity are key when delving into the grant application instructions, where understanding what the funder really wants to support is often hidden. Then, my communication skills and attention to detail come together to create the best possible application. I am pleased to report that well over 85% of the grants I have applied for have been funded.

I offer my services as a grant writer on a contract basis. Together we can make your dreams a reality!
